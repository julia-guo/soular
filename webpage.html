{soul}
ar
is a game made for Android devices where the player plays as Valentia, a girl living in the near future. In this future world, the Earth has been completely covered with a blanket of smog, and everything is just terrible. Valentia must do good deeds, some simple, some hard, in order to reverse this situation.
The game consists of four sections in increasing level of difficulty of the actual deeds that Valentia must do. The gameplay itself is simple, but the message, conveyed through subtle changes in art, scenery, and color, is effective.
▼
In
Section 1
, the player performs easy tasks that people can do to help the Earth, such as planting trees or biking instead of driving.

In
Section 2
, the difficulty of the tasks increase, and they consist of things like making donations to environmental nonprofits, hosting informational events for the community, buying an electric car, or installing solar panels.

In
Section 3
, tasks involve things that involve a great amount of commitment, like doing environmental research internships, changing one's entire heating plan to solar, or choosing to study environmental science/engineering in college.

Section 4
consists of the biggest things one can do to help the environment, like starting a solar energy startup, inventing something meaningful, or devoting one's life to environmental research.
Humans put 40 billion tons of CO2 into the air annually. At this rate, the world of this game could be our future.

Why was Soular created?

The objective is to educate people about environmental issues and how to prevent them in a fun and engaging way.

What environmental issues are there?

Pollution (Water, Soil, Air), Climate Change, Overcrowding of Land Fills

How can we prevent these issues from getting worse?

3 R's-Reduce, Reuse and Recycle, Bike or Walk, Plant Trees

Why should we care?

The world is growing more and more polluted, and we, the next generation, are the ones that will live long enough to be greatly affected by the steadily worsening environment.
